Laser Metal Cutting

SASTI LASER METAL CUTTING has been at the forefront of precision metal cutting technology for over 25 years. We specialize in providing high-quality laser cutting services for various metals including stainless steel, aluminum, copper, and mild steel. Our state-of-the-art equipment and experienced team ensure that every project meets the highest standards of precision and quality.

Advantages of Laser Cutting

Superior precision, clean edges, minimal material waste, and the ability to cut complex shapes with exceptional accuracy.

Services Offered

CO2 laser cutting, fiber laser cutting, galvo laser cutting, plasma cutting, drilling, and custom metal fabrication services.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality control processes and continuous monitoring to ensure every cut meets industry standards and client expectations.
